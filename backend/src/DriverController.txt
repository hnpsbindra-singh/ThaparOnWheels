GET    /api/driver/rides/pending  [DONE]
→ View all pending ride requests.

GET    /api/driver/rides/{rideId}  [DONE]
→ View details of a specific ride.

PATCH  /api/driver/rides/{rideId}/accept  [DONE]
→ Accept a pending ride.

GET    /api/driver/rides/active [DONE]
→ View all active (accepted) rides assigned to the driver.

GET    /api/driver/rides/completed [DONE]
→ View completed ride history.
